SUSHI

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Sushi-grade fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Soy sauce, for dipping
  • Pickled ginger and wasabi, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Rinse sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Cook according to package instructions.
  2. While the rice is still warm, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Gently fold the mixture into the rice and let it cool.
  3. Place a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a flat surface, cover it with plastic wrap, and place a sheet of nori on top.
  4. Wet your hands and spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  5. Place strips of fish, cucumber, and avocado along the bottom edge of the nori.
  6. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat. Seal the edge with a little water.
  7. Using a sharp knife, slice the roll into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  9. Serve sushi with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
