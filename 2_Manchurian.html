MANCHURIAN

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp chili sauce
  • 1/4 cup spring onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup capsicum, chopped
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Make a batter using all-purpose flour, cornstarch, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Dip cauliflower florets in the batter and deep fry until golden brown.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions, capsicum, and spring onions.
  4. Add tomato ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add fried cauliflower to the sauce and toss well.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
