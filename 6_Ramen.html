RAMEN

Ingredients:

  • 4 packs of ramen noodles
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • Sliced green onions and nori for garnish
  • Sesame seeds for topping
  • Chili oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, soy sauce, and mirin. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until they soften.
  5. Stir in the baby spinach until wilted.
  6. Divide the cooked ramen noodles into bowls and ladle the hot broth over them.
  7. Top with boiled eggs, sliced green onions, nori, sesame seeds, and chili oil if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Japanese Ramen!
