DOSA

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds. Soak them in water separately for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind the soaked rice and urad dal separately into a smooth batter. Mix both batters together, add salt, and let it ferment for 8-12 hours or until it doubles in volume.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or a griddle over medium heat. Pour a ladle of batter onto the center and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin pancake (dosa).
  4. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa and cook until the bottom turns golden brown and crispy.
  5. Flip the dosa and cook the other side for a minute or until it is cooked through.
  6. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  7. Serve dosas hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
