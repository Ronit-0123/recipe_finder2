SHAHI PANEER

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cashews, soaked
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grind soaked cashews into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, add chopped onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add cashew paste, cream, and milk. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add paneer, cardamom powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh cream and serve hot.
