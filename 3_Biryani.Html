BIRYANI

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • Whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  3. Add whole spices and ginger-garlic paste to the remaining onions. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken, turmeric powder, red chili powder, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until chicken is browned.
  5. Layer half of the partially cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle mint and coriander leaves.
  6. Spread the remaining rice over the first layer. Pour yogurt evenly over the rice.
  7. Top with the reserved sautéed onions and sprinkle biryani masala.
  8. Pour water around the edges. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  9. Fluff the biryani with a fork, mix gently, and serve hot.
